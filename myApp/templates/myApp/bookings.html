Everything You Need to Know Before Booking

How long is each session?

Sessions are available in three different lengths: 60 minutes, 90 minutes, and 120 minutes. Choose the one that best suits your needs and the depth of the guidance you seek.

How do I pay for a session?

You pay for your session afterwards. I will provide payment details after our meeting, and you can complete the transaction conveniently. You can pay via bank transfer, PayPal, or Bizum, whichever is easiest for you.

What happens during a session?

During your session, we’ll dive deep into your energy field. I’ll begin by tuning into your past, present, and future. If necessary, we may revisit key moments from the past, but the main focus will always be the present and the future. You’ll have plenty of time to ask questions, and I often address them intuitively before they’re even spoken!

Can I reschedule my appointment?

Yes, you can reschedule, but I kindly ask that you do so at least 24 hours in advance. If you need to reschedule later than that, I’ll need to charge a fee of 50% of the session price.

In which languages do you offer sessions?

I offer sessions in Dutch, Flemish, English, and German, allowing for flexibility and comfort in communication.

How do I book a session?

You can book a session by contacting me via the email link or book here with your name and email address. Once I receive your request, I’ll get back to you with available dates and times. Please make sure to include where you live so we can account for any time zone differences when scheduling the session.

What platforms do you use for sessions?

I conduct sessions via WhatsApp, Skype, Zoom, and Google Meet.
